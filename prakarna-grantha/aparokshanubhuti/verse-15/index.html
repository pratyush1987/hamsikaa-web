Enquiry - What is the material cause?

एतयोर्यदुपादानम् एकं सूक्ष्मं सदव्ययम् ।
यथैव मृद्घटादीनां विचारः सोऽयमीदृशः ॥१५॥

As the clay is the material cause for pots, so too, the one immutable, subtle Existence (brahman) is the material cause for these two (ignorance and desire). This is the way of the enquiry.


Language aid

यथैवयथा एव
↳ यथा as
↳ एव alone, only

यथैव घटादीनां (उपादानं) मृत् (भवति, तथैव) एतयो: यत् उपादानं (तत्) एकं सूक्ष्मं अव्ययं सत् (अस्ति) - विचार: सोऽयमीदृश:

Commentaries

The material (cause) of these two (i.e. ignorance and thought) is the one* (without a second), subtle (not apprehended by the senses) and unchanging Sat (Existence), just as the earth is the material (cause) of the pot and the like. This is the way of that Vicāra.

*One—Because it does not admit of a second of the same or of a different kind, or of any parts within itself. It is one homogeneous whole.

सं. टी. अथोपादानं किमस्तीत्यस्य निर्णयमाह एतयोरिति एतयोरज्ञानसंकल्पयोर्यदुपादानं उत्पत्तिस्थितिनाशाय कारणं तत्तु सत्कालत्रयाबाध्यं ब्रह्मैव नान्यदित्यर्थः अत एवाधिष्ठानज्ञाननिर्वर्त्त्याज्ञानकार्यत्वेन मिथ्याभूतमपि जगत् यावज्ज्ञानोदयं रज्जुसर्पादिवत् संसारभयव्यवहारक्षमं भवॆदितिभावः ब्राह्मणः सत्त्वेहेतुः अव्ययमिति अव्ययमपक्षयरहितं अनेनैतत्पूर्वभूताअपि जन्मादिविकारा निरस्ताः नाशश्च निरस्तः षडभावविकारराहित्ये हेतुः एकं सजातीयादिभेदशून्यं ताद्ध कुतो न दृश्यते तत्राह सूक्ष्ममिति सूक्ष्मं मनोवा गादींद्रियागोचरं तेषां प्रवृत्तिनिमित्तजातिक्रियादिशून्यत्वादित्यर्थः ब्रह्मण उपादानत्वे दृष्टांतमाह यथैवेति यथैव मृत्घटादीनामुपादानं तथैवेत्यर्थः एवंप्रकारेण कार्यकारणभेदो नाममात्रमिति सूचितम् ॥१६॥

भा. टी. जैसे मृत्तिका घटका उपादान कारणहै और सुवर्ण कुण्डलादिका कारणहै इसी प्रकार जो एक ‘सूक्ष्म’ सत्यस्वरूप, अविनाशी अज्ञान ‘सङ्कल्प’ इनका जो उपादान कारण है सोई इस जगत्का उपदान कारणहै इसका जो निरूपण है सो विचार कहावे है ॥ १५॥


Reference Material

Swami Sarvapriyananda lecture in En

  • What is the material cause? Upon inspection, a pot is found to be made up of clay or mud. Thus, the material cause is mud which has only been transformed by the efficient cause - the potter. The potter does not create material, he only transforms it.
  • What is the material cause of ignorance and thought? Ignorance and thought have been identified to the efficient cause of this universe that we experience. There material cause is the सत् (sat) or Existence or brahman. It is decribed as being subtle (सूक्ष्म sUkshma) and immutable (अव्यय avyaya).
  • How is existence the material cause? Whatever exists is postulated on the ground of existence. Indeed, existence is causeless. For that which is the cause of existence can exist not. Existence is the causeless substratum of all that is.
  • What are the properties of existence? It is एकं (ekam) - singular, सूक्ष्मं (sUkshmam) - subtle, and अव्ययं (avyayam) - immutable.
    • Singular: Existence cannot be differentiated, for what would exist between two variants of existence? Hence existence is singular - the One reality.
    • Subtle: The property of existence has no connection with any sensed property of an object. Existence is independent and subtle.
    • Immutable: Existence cannot be transformed from one state to the other. For if that occured, who would be the cause or witness of this transformation? That alone exists.
